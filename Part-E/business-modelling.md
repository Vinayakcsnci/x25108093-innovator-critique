## Pre-Mortem: Failure in 12 Months (Approach d)

Imagine it is April 2027 and MangoMind has failed. The obituary reads:

*"MangoMind closed this week after a DPC investigation found its employee monitoring system non-compliant with GDPR and potentially in breach of the EU AI Act's Annex III employment provisions. The company had launched without completing an independent conformity assessment, relying on a self-declaration of limited-risk status. The investigation was triggered by a complaint from a SIPTU member whose employer had used MangoMind data in a redundancy consultation. Despite €2M in funding and early traction, the trust deficit — the very problem the product claimed to solve — destroyed the business from the inside."*

**Three named failure modes:**
1. **Regulatory misclassification** — shipping under incorrect AI Act risk category
2. **Trust betrayal at first adverse event** — any use of the data outside its stated purpose (even by a customer, not MangoMind) becomes MangoMind's reputational problem
3. **Clinical overclaiming** — a customer acts on a false-positive BRI alert, the employee suffers harm, and MangoMind faces negligence exposure

---

## The Three Non-Negotiable Conditions

**Condition 1: EU AI Act conformity assessment completed and documented (Legal)**
*Why non-negotiable:* Without this, shipping in Ireland is a regulatory gamble. A DPC complaint would halt operations and erase the trust capital the entire product is built on. Timeline: 90 days with external legal counsel retained immediately. Cost: included in the €90K legal/compliance budget.

**Condition 2: IBEC/ICTU joint engagement completed; model contract clause agreed (Governance)**
*Why non-negotiable:* Irish workplace law requires consultation with employee representatives for changes to working conditions monitoring. Without a model clause that unions have reviewed, MangoMind cannot be deployed in any unionised workplace — which is a substantial fraction of the Irish SMB market. Timeline: 60–90 days. Cost: €10K legal facilitation.

**Condition 3: All statistical claims in artefacts independently verified and corrected (Credibility)**
*Why non-negotiable:* The €11B figure, the 78% IBEC statistic, and the 94% retention metric are all either wrong or misleading. An investor who conducts diligence and finds fabricated statistics will withdraw the offer and brief the market. Fix every claim before close. Timeline: 2 weeks. Cost: zero — a desk research exercise.

**Ship date with conditions met: 90–120 days from today (July–August 2026).**

No hedging: if conditions 1 and 2 are not met by Day 90, do not ship. A compliant MangoMind that ships at Day 120 is worth infinitely more than a non-compliant one that faces enforcement at Day 91.

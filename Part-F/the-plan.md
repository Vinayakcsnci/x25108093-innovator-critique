## What We Are Deliberately NOT Doing (and Why)

| Not doing | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| **UK market expansion before Day 180** | Ireland is the proof. Entering UK before we have Irish case studies and a validated product risks spreading resources before the model is proven. UK can wait until Series A. |
| **Enterprise (250+ employee) sales** | SMB is the wedge. Enterprise procurement cycles are 6–18 months; we cannot afford the CAC in Year 1. Enterprise is Year 3. |
| **Building a proprietary AI model from scratch** | We use fine-tuned BERT and federated learning frameworks that exist. Custom model training is a Year 2 investment after we have training data from real customers. |
| **Acquiring any company** | €2M is a building budget, not an M&A budget. Any acquisition conversation before Series A is a distraction. |
| **Hiring a PR/comms agency** | We'll handle launch PR directly with the CEO's network and a freelance PR consultant. Agency retainers of €8–12K/month are wasteful at this stage. |
| **Paying dividends or salary premiums to founders** | Founders take market-rate salaries (€85K pa). Every euro of the raise goes into the product and compliance. |

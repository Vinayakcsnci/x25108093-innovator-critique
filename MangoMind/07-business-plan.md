# MangoMind – Business Plan

**Pipeline Step 7**

## Executive Summary

MangoMind is an AI-powered employee burnout risk intelligence platform targeting Irish SMBs (10–250 employees). We track anonymised team-level behavioural signals from workplace tools to surface burnout risk 2–3 weeks before crisis — giving HR managers time to intervene. We are seeking €2 million seed investment to build the product, achieve initial commercial traction, and establish regulatory compliance infrastructure.

## Regulatory Posture

MangoMind is a **limited-risk AI system** under the EU AI Act (Article 50 transparency obligations apply). It does **not** fall under Annex III high-risk categories because it does not make decisions about employment status, does not infer protected characteristics, and does not produce individual-level outputs. A DPIA under GDPR Article 35 is required and will be conducted pre-launch. Irish DPC guidance will be sought proactively.

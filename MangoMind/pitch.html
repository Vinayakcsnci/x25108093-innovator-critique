The Insight

Burnout leaves a digital signal trail weeks before crisis

Behavioural science and occupational psychology research demonstrate that burnout progression correlates with measurable changes in digital work patterns — increased after-hours messaging, meeting overload, declining response latency, sentiment degradation in communications.


The Dolphin Model

Bottlenose dolphin pods detect individual stress through distributed micro-signals weeks before visible illness. No single signal is conclusive — the pattern is. MangoMind applies the same distributed-signal logic to workplace data.

The Lead Time Opportunity

Research (Bakker & Demerouti, 2017; Maslach & Leiter, 2016) identifies a 2–4 week detectable signal window before clinical burnout onset. That window is the product.

The Solution

MangoMind: Team-level burnout intelligence

1. Anonymous Signal Capture

Integrates with Slack, Teams, and Google Calendar. Captures metadata only — timing, frequency, density. Message content is never read.

2. On-Device Processing

Raw signals processed on-device. Only aggregated, k-anonymised team vectors leave the processing boundary. GDPR-compliant by architecture.

3. Burnout Risk Index

AI computes a Burnout Risk Index (BRI, 0–100) benchmarked against that team's own rolling 12-week baseline. No cross-company comparisons.

4. HR Dashboard

Plain-language recommendations for HR managers. No individual names ever shown. Threshold alerts. EAP resource links.

5. Intervention Library

Curated, evidence-based team interventions mapped to BRI severity levels. Manager guidance, not clinical advice.

6. 10-Minute Setup

OAuth-based integration. No IT department required. Live in under 10 minutes. Mobile-first dashboard, accessible anywhere.

Privacy & Compliance

Privacy is the product, not a feature

What MangoMind NEVER does:

  • ✗  Read message content
  • ✗  Show individual employee scores to HR
  • ✗  Store raw behavioural data beyond 24 hours
  • ✗  Infer protected characteristics
  • ✗  Make employment decisions

Regulatory compliance stack:

  • GDPR Article 6(1)(f) — Legitimate interests basis; DPIA completed
  • EU AI Act — Limited-risk classification; Article 50 transparency notices deployed to all employees
  • Irish Employment Law — No performance assessment use case; no industrial relations trigger
  • Data hosting — AWS eu-west-1 Dublin; no data leaves EU

Key differentiator: Our privacy architecture is a hard constraint, not a configuration. Competitors can turn off privacy protections. MangoMind cannot — it's in the code.

How MangoMind Works

Passive signals. Anonymous data. Early warning.

MangoMind never reads your messages. We only analyse timing and metadata — the same signals a good manager notices intuitively, made visible and consistent.

1

Connect Your Tools

One-click OAuth integration with Slack, Microsoft Teams, and Google Calendar. Setup takes under 10 minutes.

2

We Track Signals, Not Content

MangoMind analyses message timing, calendar density, after-hours patterns, and optional weekly pulse check-ins. Message content is never read.

Our AI models each team's unique normal — not a cross-company comparison. Deviations from your team's own pattern trigger alerts.

Privacy & Compliance

Built for GDPR. Designed against surveillance.

🔒

Zero Individual Exposure — By Architecture

MangoMind never shows HR managers individual-level scores. Data is aggregated using k-anonymity (minimum group size: 5) before leaving your device. No employee can be identified from MangoMind output. This is a hard architectural constraint — not a configuration option. Our system is fully GDPR-compliant and operates under a completed Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA).

GDPR Article 6 Compliant

Legal basis: legitimate interests + informed consent. All employees receive a transparency notice before deployment. Right to erasure fully supported.

EU AI Act Ready

MangoMind is classified as a limited-risk AI system. Transparency obligations under Article 50 are met via mandatory employee disclosure notices.

Hosted in Ireland

All data processed and stored on AWS eu-west-1 (Dublin). No data leaves the EU. ISO 27001 certification in progress.
